1 Select FLAC

Two-pass EBU R128 to your Settings target. Tags and artwork are copied to the new file. Audio is 16-bit, same sample rate and channel layout as the source. Normalised masters often produce larger FLACs than the original (same rate/bit-depth) because the waveform is harder for FLAC to compress — that is normal and still lossless. With Xiph flac installed (brew install flac), encoding uses flac --best -e -p (slower, tighter). Otherwise FFmpeg’s FLAC encoder is used. Output: filename_suffix.flac in the same folder.
