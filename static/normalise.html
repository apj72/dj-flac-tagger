1 Select audio

Two-pass EBU R128 to your Settings target. Tags and artwork are copied to the new file. Output format and extension come from Settings → extract format (default FLAC). For FLAC: audio is 16-bit at the source sample rate and layout; normalised files are often larger than the original because the waveform is harder to compress — that is normal. With Xiph flac installed, FLAC encoding uses flac --best -e -p. For MP3 / AAC, FFmpeg encodes after loudnorm. Output: filename_suffix plus the configured extension, in the same folder.
