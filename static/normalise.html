1 Select audio Two-pass EBU R128 to your Settings target. Tags and artwork are copied to the new file. Output format and file extension come from Settings → extract format (FLAC, MP3, or AAC/M4A; default FLAC). For FLAC output: audio is 16-bit at the source sample rate and layout; files are often larger than the original because lossless waveforms are harder to compress — that is expected. With Xiph flac installed, FLAC encoding uses flac --best -e -p. For MP3 / AAC, FFmpeg encodes after loudnorm. Writes {stem}{suffix} plus the configured extension beside the original.
